Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is Fluid101?

A: Fluid101 is a health tool that provides evidence-based oral rehydration solutions for different conditions like diarrhea, vomiting, fever, sweating, and burns.

Q: Who can use this app?

A: This app is suitable for use by laypersons, caregivers, and healthcare workers. It is designed to work for all age groups including infants, children, adults, and elderly.

Q: Does Fluid101 work offline?

A: Yes. Once installed, all rehydration recipes and guidance are available offline.

Q: Is this a substitute for medical advice?

A: No. Fluid101 is for educational and supportive use only. It does not replace professional medical consultation or emergency care.

Q: Why was I asked to grant location permissions?

A: In future versions, Fluid101 may include location-based features to guide users to nearby emergency facilities. For now, no location data is used or collected.
